Uranus

The Ice Giant is about 4 times bigger than Earth and can hold about 63 Earths inside it!

Rotation

One day on Uranus takes about 17 hours. Complete orbit around the Sun takes about 84 Earth years

Moons

The ice giant is surrounded by 13 faint rings and 28 small moons.

Life?

Uranus' environment is not conducive to life as we know it. The temperatures, pressures, and materials that characterize this planet are most likely too extreme and volatile for organisms to adapt to.

Disovery

The first planet discovered with the help of a telescope. It was discovered in 1781 by astronomer William Herschel. It was two years later that the objectwas universally accepted as a new planet, in part because of observations by astronomer Johann Elert Bode.
