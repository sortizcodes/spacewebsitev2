Mars

The Red Planet is about half the size of Earth.

Rotation

24.6 hours.

Two small Moons

Phobos and Deimos.

Most explored Planet

It's the only planet where we've sent rovers to roam the alien landscape.

Temperature

As high as 70 degrees Fahrenheit (20 degrees Celsius) or as low as about -225 degrees Fahrenheit (-153 degrees Celsius).

Mars Colors

Mars looks reddish is due to oxidization or rusting of iron in the rocks. This is called regolith (Martian “soil”), and dust of Mars.
