Mission Briefing: The Solar System

Ready to explore the 8 planets in our Milky Way galaxy?

Introduction

Our solar system is located inside the Milky Way, a spiral galaxy with a sun, eight planets, five dwarf planets, hundreds of moons and even thousands of asteroids and comets! Now you’re probably wondering... what is a planet? The word dates back to the ancient Greek word planēt, which means “wanderer.” A modern definition can be found in the Merriam-Webster dictionary, which defines a planet as “any of the large bodies that revolve around the Sun in the solar system.” Please read on to learn more!

Origin

Formed 4.6 billion years ago from a solar nebulla.

Structure

Includes 4 inner and 4 outer planets.

Home
