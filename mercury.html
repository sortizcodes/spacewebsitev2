Mercury

This fast planet is about the size of a peppercorn.

Rotation

Always in a hurry, zipping around the Sun every 88 Earth days.

Moons

No moons.

Next to the sun

Mercury is the closest planet to the sun.

Temperature

Extreme mood swings! Scorching hot at 800°F (430°C) during the day and freezing at -290°F (-180°C) at night.

Mercury's surface

Mercury has a central core, a rocky mantle, and a solid crust. Mercury's surface resembles that of Earth's Moon.
